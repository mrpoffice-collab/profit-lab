Profit Lab

Privacy Policy

Effective July 14, 2026 · Profit Lab is a product of Code63 Labs

What we collect

  • Jobber business data (with your permission, via Jobber's API): jobs, visits, invoices, expenses, and your business name — the inputs the analysis needs.
  • Your email address, when you subscribe, so we can deliver your reports.
  • Payment details are collected and stored by Stripe, our payment processor. We never see or store your card number.

What we do with it

One thing: compute your profit analysis and deliver it to you. We store your connection tokens (encrypted in transit and at rest on our database provider), the computed metrics, and your generated reports so you can revisit them.

What we never do

  • We never sell your data, or anyone's.
  • We never contact your clients. Their information passes through our analysis only to compute your per-client profitability and is never used for marketing — ours or anyone else's.
  • We never modify anything in your Jobber account.
  • We don't run third-party ad trackers on our pages.

Service providers we rely on

Render (application and database hosting), Stripe (payments), Anthropic (the AI that writes report narratives from your computed numbers), Resend (email delivery), and Jobber (the source of your data, under your authorization). Each receives only what its role requires.

Deletion

Disconnect Profit Lab from Jobber's App Marketplace at any time — access tokens are revoked immediately. Email mrpoffice@gmail.com to have your stored data and reports deleted entirely; we complete deletion requests within 30 days.
